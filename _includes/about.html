About

Born just outside New York, I’ve been dead-set on working in the mortuary sciences since birth. A natural tinkerer, I began burial services for live cats at age 9. For years, I’ve killed the competition with my skills in corpse Embalming and preparation.

After taking a short sabbatical building a tarantuala farm on my family’s estate, I’ve returned to my passion for the deceased as a freelancer. Past examples of my event management and cremation work include the play production of the original Thanksgiving at Camp Chippewa.
